# Comparison Site Outreach Strategy

*Created: 2026-02-06*

## Why This Matters

Comparison/affiliate sites have strong SEO authority on keywords like "logiciel facturation auto-entrepreneur" and "facture électronique 2026". Getting listed can drive significant traffic at zero advertising cost.

---

## Priority Targets

### 1. facture-electronique-obligatoire.fr ⭐⭐⭐

**Type:** Affiliate/comparator site (NOT a competitor)
**Claims:** 12,000+ businesses, 50+ platforms listed
**Traffic:** Strong SEO on "facture électronique obligatoire"

**How They Work:**
- Compare e-invoicing platforms and refer users
- Commission-based (affiliate model)
- Already list: Pennylane, Indy, Sage, Quickbooks, etc.

**Our Angle:**
- €9/mo is cheaper than most they recommend
- Specifically designed for auto-entrepreneurs
- Free tools (invoice generator, checklist) as differentiator
- 100% French-made

**Action:**
1. Contact form or email: request to be added to comparator
2. Offer to provide demo account for review
3. Highlight: price, simplicity, AE-focus

---

### 2. Selectra (selectra.info) ⭐⭐⭐

**Article:** selectra.info/finance/guides/banque-pro/logiciel-facturation/auto-entrepreneur
**Title:** "Top 6 des logiciels de facturation pour auto-entrepreneurs"
**Currently Lists:** Indy, Abby, Henrri, Tiime, etc.

**Our Angle:**
- FacturSimple NOT currently listed → opportunity!
- Similar target audience (auto-entrepreneurs)
- Competitive pricing (€9/mo vs €10-30/mo for others)

**Action:**
1. Find editorial contact for finance section
2. Pitch FacturSimple as a new option worth adding
3. Offer interview or guest article about 2026 reform

---

### 3. Independant.io ⭐⭐

**Content:** Vertical comparisons (e.g., "6 Meilleurs Logiciels Devis-Facture Plomberie")
**Style:** Niche-specific guides for freelancers

**Our Angle:**
- They write niche guides - we have niche blog articles
- Could partner on content or be listed in guides
- Good for long-tail SEO traffic

**Action:**
1. Identify relevant comparison articles
2. Request inclusion with our niche pages as evidence of expertise

---

### 4. Appvizer.fr ⭐⭐

**Type:** B2B software comparison platform
**Audience:** Businesses looking for software solutions

**Action:**
1. Create an Appvizer listing for FacturSimple
2. Encourage reviews from early users
3. Monitor competitor reviews for insights

### 5. Les Echos Entrepreneurs / Le Figaro Entreprises ⭐
